Production Notes - Scene 10 - The Most Difficult of Duties

We return briefly back to the homefront to Major Mayweather, who is given the task of informing Stephanie of her loss. The latter half of this scene was done without any dialog. Part of the reason for this is I'm not confident in writing dialog for this sort of scene. ;) I think, though, that it works quite well just seeing it unfold from a distance; the viewer's imagination can fill in what's actually being said.

The music switches from the first portion of The Last Stand to the last portion of Adagio, ending with the final violin cue matching Stephanie sitting with their dog Bruno.
