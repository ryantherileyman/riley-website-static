Production Notes - Opening Credits

The short pre-credits sequence provides a little bit of "historical information" (partially fictional), and then we have the opening title and credits. The four main characters, and three secondary characters, appear in the opening.

Musical cues you may not have noticed:

  • "Dark Horizon" blends into the last portion of "Magic of the Movies".
  • The simple lens flare effect on the "R" in "Riley Entertainment" was timed for a specific violin cue.
  • The film title was timed for the drum cue.
  • The "starring" word was timed for the triangle cue.
  • Lance and Stephanie facing the camera was timed for the flute cue.

Additional editing you may not have noticed:

  • The only moment when ambient noise is heard is when Lance and Stephanie appear. The specific wind noise was chosen by exporting that scene alone and cropping it to the portion I wanted. The leaves blowing acts as some subtle foreshadowing for their relationship.
