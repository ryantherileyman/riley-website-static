For My Fellow Soldier is now available on DVD!

In order to make this film available on DVD, I used WinRAR to split the file into five pieces and placed them on tmo-hi-res.com for download. Download WinRAR (for PCs) or UnRarX (for Macs) to extract the DVD image file, once you have downloaded all five pieces below. Then use your favourite DVD burning software to create the DVD. It will play on your computer, as well as any DVD player!
