Production Notes - Closing Credits

The closing credits include a tribute to the Armed Forces of the Allies, some historical information, and a complete list of credits for the cast and "crew". The flag animations were gathered from fg-a.com, though it did take some effort to transfer them into animated overlays. ;) The five flags are those of the United Kingdom, Poland, France, Canada, and the United States - the same flags, in the same order, as they appear at the actual monument in Saint-Lambert-sur-Dives in Normandy.
