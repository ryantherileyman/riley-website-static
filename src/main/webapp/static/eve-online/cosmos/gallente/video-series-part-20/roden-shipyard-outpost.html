Room 1 - The Shipyard

The first room of this site is home to a handful of Serpentis pirates, protecting a shipyard and other structures. The acceleration gate into the next area is not locked, and there is no special loot.

Room 2 - The Pirate Haven

The second room of this site is guarded by seven groups of Serpentis. There may be an Angel Cartel pirate named Maqeri Camcen in the distance. The acceleration gate into the next area is not locked.

Room 3 - Corelum Drughouse

The third and final room of this site is guarded by both Serpentis and Rogue Drones. Amongst the Serpentis is a pirate named Jark Makon. In the distance is the wreckage of Isone's Ship. The Serpentis Narcotics Storage structure drops a 3rd-Tier Overseer's Effects, and may drop random Coreli C-Type deadspace modules.
