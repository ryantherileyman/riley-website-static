Career Agents and Data Centers

The career agents are a worthwhile activity for all new pilots, or for pilots who are looking to repair their standings with one of the four main factions.

The data center agents provide a way of gaining faction standings quickly. Each typically requires 20 pirate dog tags, which can be acquired either by destroying NPC pirates (random chance), or off the market (the easy way).
