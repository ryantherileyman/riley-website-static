Entry Gate

The entry gate to this site is guarded by a group of Friends of Nature activists, led by Apte Donie. Apte Donie drops an Azure Canyon Tourist Pass, which is used to access the acceleration gate.

2x Friends of Nature Cruisers
Apte Donie
 Apte Donie drops an Azure Canyon Tourist Pass

Room 1 - The Tourist Resort

The first room of this site has four Pleasure Gardens domes guarded by three groups of Serpentis. There may be a Friends of Nature activist named Alarus Ekire amongst the Serpentis, flying a Megathron. The acceleration gate into the next area is not locked.

Room 2 - The Azure Stone

Warping to the second room of this site puts you in the midst of a group of Serpentis cruisers. Surrounding the Azure Stone above the entry beacon are three more groups of Serpentis, led by Daubs Louel. The acceleration gate to the right, to the Ion Field, is locked, requiring you to destroy all hostile NPCs. The second acceleration gate ahead, to the Black Market, is also locked, requiring the Black Market Entry Keycard.

Room 3 - The Ion Field

The third room of this site is home to a Level 4 Mining Cosmos Agent named Ampsin Achippon. Four Friends of Nature heavy missile batteries guard the ion gas clouds.

Group Location Ship Types Notes
2 Groups Right of Entry
2 groups of 2x Friends of Nature Heavy Missile Battery
Level 4 Cosmos Agent Ampsin Achippon

Room 4 - The Black Market

The fourth and final room of this site is guarded by a large group of Serpentis surrounding a Serpentis Stronghold. The nearest group of Serpentis is guarding a prison facility filled with Temko Mercenaries. The Serpentis Stronghold drops a Serpentis Transaction Log, and may drop a storyline module blueprint.
