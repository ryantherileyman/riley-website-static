Room 1 - Wiyrkomi Engineering Outpost

The first room of this site is guarded by a sizeable collection of Serpentis, possibly led by a Wiyrkomi Head Engineer. The Wiyrkomi Head Engineer drops a 7th-Tier Overseer's Effects, and may drop random Shadow Serpentis faction modules. The acceleration gate into the next area is locked, requiring you to destroy all hostile NPCs.

Room 2 - Infested Comet Remains

The second room of this site is guarded by a group of Rogue Drones on the left, and a group of Serpentis on the right. There are two completely separate Serpentis spawns possible, and they may be led by The Negotiator. The Negotiator drops an 8th-Tier Overseer's Effects, and may drop random Shadow Serpentis faction modules.

Room 3 - Remote Corelum Safe Haven

The third and final room of this site is guarded by four groups of Serpentis, each near a Fire Cloud Harvester. There may be a Core Serpentis Operational Headquarters, possibly guarded by two HQ Guardian battlecruisers. The Headquarters drops a 9th-Tier Overseer's Effects, and may drop random Coreli A-Type deadspace modules.
