Room 1 - The Skeleton Comet

The first room of this site is home to the giant skeleton comet, with a large structure named the All-You-Desire Haven guarded by Serpentis. A group of Serpentis Junkies flying frigates drop items marked Stolen Goods. A pair of Scorched Containers guarded by a Serpentis Knucklebuster drops Spoiled Drugs. Neither acceleration gate is locked.

Room 2 - Drone Infested Corridor

The second room of this site is guarded by a large group of rogue drones on the left, and a group of Serpentis on the right. Splitting the room in the distance are a group of FON activists guarding a Friends of Nature Operation Station, which drops an FON Banner. Amongst the rogue drones, the Drone Workers drop Neural Bio Links, and the Drone Perimeter Guards drop Aether Hive Links. The acceleration gate is locked, requiring all hostile NPCs to be destroyed.

It is difficult, but possible, to solo this room. Destroying the FON Operation Station prevents the FON activists in group 2 from respawning. Destroying the two Vast Drone Hive structures prevents groups B and C from respawning. The remaining rogue drones respawn after a few minutes, so you must draw them close to the acceleration gate before engaging them.

Room 3 - Remote Corelum Safe Haven

The third room of this site is guarded by two groups of Serpentis. The group on the left surround a Runner's Relay Station, which drops a ComLink Encoder/Decoder. The group on the right guard a Serpentis Command Outpost, which drops a Strike Force Gear.

Room 4 - Serpentis Survey Site

The fourth room of this site has two groups of Serpentis Caskets that can be hacked using a Data Analyzer. The group guarding the Caskets on the right are led by a single Serpentis battleship and two elite frigates that may use a warp disruptor. The group guarding the (much more difficult to hack) Caskets on the left are led by two Serpentis battleships and four elite frigates. The Caskets contain various Serpentis materials, as well as the potential for a Threaded Waypoint Map. The acceleration gate is locked, and requires a Smuggler Knot Lock, which may drop from any of the Serpentis Surveyor cruisers.

Room 5 - Ancient Ruins

The fifth and final room of this site has a large group of Yan Jung containers that can be hacked using a Relic Analyzer. The containers have a variety of Yan Jung materials, as well as the Yan Jung Technology skill book. The room is guarded by many Serpentis, led by two battleships and three elite frigates.
